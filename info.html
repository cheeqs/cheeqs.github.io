Commands

  • /trash - Open an inventory to delete trash
  • /home - Takes you to the bed you've set for your spawn point. If you haven't set a bed, it will take you to world spawn.
  • /back - Takes you to the last place you teleported from.
  • /astools - Replace inventory with armor stand tools, type again to restore inventory
  • /craft - Opens up the 3x3 crafting grid without using a crafting table.
  • /condense - Compresses most compressable items in your inventory, e.g. ingots to blocks
  • /itf togglemode - shift right click item frames to make them invisible
  • /es - edit sign mode, shift right click with an empty hand on sign to edit
  • /beezooka and /kittycannon - For fun
  • /kit - get various items with a cooldown, options are "color", "notch", and "firework"
  • {item} - displays the item held in your hand to chat.
  • //wand - gives you a wooden axe that you can use to set points for claims
  • /region claim <claim name> - claims the area selected by your wand (hint: get the WorldEditCUI mod)
  • /warp tutorial - Warps you to a short tutorial on how to begin with slimefun
  • CHEST LOCKING - Chests, doors, furnaces, fence gates etc... can be locked by shift right clicking with an empty hand. and selecting "lock". You can add players to the whitelist by clicking "Manage Friends", "Search player" (bottom right), typing in the name to search in the text bar and clicking the renamed paper that it makes.

Economy

Chest Shops

Buying - To purchase from a chest shop, right click the sign. You must have enough funds to purchase from any chest shop. You may also right click the container to view more information on what's being sold or bought.

Selling - To create a chest shop, edit a sign on the shop container in this format: first line blank, second line quantity of item, third line ("B" or "S") "cost of item", fourth line "?" then click done. Example:

Jobs & Earning

To earn money, type "/jobs browse" and right click up to 3 jobs to select them. Once you've done this, you may start earning money by doing specific tasks.

In the "/jobs browse" menu, left click the job you chose and hover over the icons. This will show you your job tasks and how much they pay out.

To check your balance, type /bal (this money is the server currency and what we will be using for the chest shops in the shopping district)

Money usage

Claims do not cost anything on this server.

"/ah" will bring up the auction house. This will show items sold by other players.

"/warp shop" will teleport you to the shopping district, where player shops and NPC shops sell various goods for server currency.

1.19 info

With the release of 1.19, the world (which was generated pre-1.19) was expanded to accomodate new blocks and structures.

All blocks in a 7000 block radius (-7000, -7000 to 7000, 7000) are pre-generated and only contain 1.18.2 and below blocks.

All blocks outside of the 7000 block radius are not generated, and will generate new 1.19 biomes and structures when loaded.

The server has had trouble in the past loading many new 1.19 chunks at once, such as flying with elytra. Expect slight to severe lag if you do this, and be considerate of other players who are online.

There is currently a portal in the shopping district to a small pre-loaded portion of 1.19 world.

Bundles are sold by an NPC in the NPC shop (small red and spruce shop straight ahead of portal)
